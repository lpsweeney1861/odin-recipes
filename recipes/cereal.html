Cereal, Because it's Easy

How it's done

Cereal has been selected for this page because it's super easy to make. You literally just pour the cereal from the box into a bowl, then pour milk into the bowl. Sweet.

Ingredients

  • Cereal of your choice
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Pour cereal into bowl
  2. Pour milk into bowl
  3. Enjoy with spoon, don't use a fork
