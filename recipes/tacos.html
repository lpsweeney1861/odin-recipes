Sweeney's Famous Turkey Tacos

How it's done

This is a quick and easy recipe that Conor Sweeney learned from his mother. The first step is to break apart your ground turkey in a well-oiled pan and cook until browned. Once browned, add your taco seasoning and 1/2 cup of water. Cook for a few more minutes. At this point you should be heating up your crunchy taco shells in the oven. Once those are done, you're good to go. Add the turkey and toppings of your choice. I typically add chopped mangos, avocado salsa with corn, shredded cheese, and hot sauce. Voila.

Ingredients

  • One package of ground turkey
  • Taco seasoning
  • Crunchy taco shells
  • Mangos
  • Canned Corn
  • 1-2 avocados
  • Shredded cheese, Mexican blend
  • Hot sauce of your choice

Instructions

  1. Prep toppings
    • Dice mangos
    • Mix corn, avocado, and lime in bowl
  2. Mix ground turkey in a well-oiled pan at medium heat
  3. Cook until browned
  4. Once browned, add taco seasoning
  5. Put crunchy taco shells in oven for 5 min
  6. Finish cooking shells and turkey, fire it up
