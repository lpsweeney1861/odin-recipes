Hot n Heavy Cocktail

This incredible cocktail was discovered by the Sweeney's at a restaurant in Bend, Oregon. It was so delicious, they insisted the bartender share the recipe with them. Now, the recipe is made known to you.

Ingredients

  • Silver tequila
  • Malibu rum
  • Jalapeno
  • Cucumber
  • Lime juice
  • Simple syrup

Instructions

  1. Muddle 1 slice of cucumber and 1 slice of jalapeno in mixing cup with fresh lime juice
  2. Add 2 oz silvertequila
  3. Add 1/4 cup of simple syrup
  4. Add ice and shake until chilled
  5. Strain into chilled martini glass and garnish with a jalapeno slice
